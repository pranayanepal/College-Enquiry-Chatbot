About the College:-
Pascal National College is a modern IT‑focused institution located in Satdobato, Lalitpur. It specializes in providing industry‑oriented education, particularly in the field of Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) under the affiliation of Tribhuvan University (TU). The college was founded and is guided by experienced academicians, IT professionals, and industry experts with the aim of preparing students for careers in software development, networking, cybersecurity, and emerging technology fields. 

Academic Programs:-

Four‑year full‑time undergraduate program
Affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU)
Emphasis on practical, project‑based learning and research
Includes real‑world IT applications and industry tools

Admission Guidelines (PNC):-
To apply for the BCA program at Pascal National College:
Complete +2 (10+2) or equivalent from any stream
Secure a minimum D+ grade in all subjects
Pass the TU BCA entrance examination conducted by the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences (FOHSS), TU
Submit application and documents before deadline
Admission is subject to seat availability (around ~35 seats)
